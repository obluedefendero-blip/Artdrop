Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ArtDrop is a simple, free drawing gallery. We do not collect any personal information, require sign-ups, or store any user data. This website exists solely for you to browse and download drawings.

1. What Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any other personal details. No account or sign-up is required to use this website.

2. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. Your browsing on ArtDrop is completely anonymous.

3. Downloads

When you download a drawing, the image file is saved directly to your device. We do not track, log, or store any information about which images you download.

4. Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect basic server access logs (IP address, browser type) as part of their standard hosting service. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Policy for details.

We use Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect data as part of serving these fonts. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

ArtDrop is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Image Rights

All images on ArtDrop are AI-generated illustrations intended for personal, non-commercial use only. By downloading, you agree to use images for personal purposes only and not for resale or commercial use.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through our GitHub repository page.
